American Tap Dance School

The first and the only American Tap Dance school in Belarus where anyone can master not only the basic technique‚ but also learn global modern tendencies of the discipline and master the improvization art. And get a positive mood portion‚ meet new friends and enter the world of diverse rhythms.

Students and teachers of the Tap Town school take an active part in various concerts‚ festivals and master–classes.

The new proposal of the dancing season of 2015-2016: "Way to Broadway" – Three times a week 7–10–year–old children and 10-16–year–old teenagers attend classes in tap dance and vocal training in a jazz interpretation. The parents can attend classes in FitDanceMix/straching in the next–door class at the same time.
